The Problem

Identity is now the primary attack surface. Most organizations still rely on static access controls and periodic reviews. Over time, privileges accumulate and roles multiply, while access is rarely removed. Policy intent quietly drifts from reality. This silent identity drift is behind most modern breaches.

Our Belief

Identity risk doesn’t appear overnight — it drifts. IDShift is an AI-driven identity security advisory that helps enterprises detect and explain identity risk across IAM, cloud access, and privileged access — all on top of your existing IAM stack without replacing it.

IDShift Reduce™: Privilege and role optimization to reduce access sprawl.

IDShift Guard™: Continuous detection of identity drift and anomalous access behavior.
